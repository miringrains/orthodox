Welcome Home to Holy Trinity

We are a vibrant Orthodox Christian community where ancient faith meets modern life. Whether you are Orthodox, curious about Orthodoxy, or simply seeking God, you are welcome here.

We gather each week for Divine Liturgy, the ancient worship service that has been celebrated for nearly two thousand years, followed by fellowship and education for all ages.

Life in the Church

01

Divine Worship

Experience the beauty and depth of Orthodox liturgical worship — ancient hymns, sacred icons, and the Holy Mysteries that have nourished Christians for millennia.

02

Living Community

We are a family of believers from all backgrounds and walks of life, united in faith and love. Join us for fellowship, service projects, and parish life.

03

Learn & Grow

Deepen your faith through catechism classes, Bible studies, Sunday school for children, and adult education exploring the riches of Orthodox theology.
